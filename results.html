Our analysis for the project:


Doctor Turk is unique because we provide financial incentives to workers by paying them to answer questions. Our motivation behind this was that it would motivate workers to answer questions and provide more comprehensive responses.


We initially set out to test the effects of pay grade and geography.


Using all of our question/answers and answer ranking data, we generated the following pie chart:


The chart shows two main things:
1. That the crowd is in fact better at answering questions than a single person
2. Monetary incentive was not a predictor in the quality of the answer.


We also generated a map showing the geographic distribution of workers on Crowdflower who answered the various questions we posted.
